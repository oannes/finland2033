# Phase 3 — Desk briefs (2031)

## PM

The Second Gate tested everything built since 2027; the country lived whatever your earlier choices made of those eleven days. The megaproject's realized savings are now a published number with claimants, the election is in the spring, and abroad the great powers discuss counting accelerators like warheads while the first ward robots ship to allied hospitals.

**The decision.** Pay the bridge, and the savings fund retraining, care and a permanent reform capacity; the books stay tight. Pay the books, and the arithmetic is impeccable and a generation reads the message. Put the question on the ballot, and the money waits while the campaign burns. Whatever you allocate is also your platform.

## SAK

This is the table everything since 2027 pointed at. Your signature is the only legitimacy the dividend can have. The first care robots abroad mean the machine is coming for your members' hands next, not just their desks; whatever rights you win now are the template for that negotiation too.

**The decision.** Sign with teeth, and retraining and gain-sharing go into law while your strike mandate retires. Refuse and strike, if the ballot is still live, and you spend labour's one credible threat weeks before an election. Settle sector by sector, and the confederation survives while the moment passes. The mandate expires with this parliament either way.

## TI

The automation levy is on the table. Your members can live with a number; they cannot live with open-ended.

**The decision.** Negotiate the levy and it arrives capped, sunset-claused, and buys labour peace your members can invoice. Fight it and win, and the precedent dies with angrier labour. Fight it and lose, and it arrives uncapped. Choose which risk is yours.

## COUNTY

Whatever is funded, you operate: retraining desks, care reinvestment, consolidation targets, or the unfunded aftermath of a punt. The first care-robot pilots abroad put an asterisk on your ten-year staffing plan that nobody can price yet.

**The decision.** Co-sign the dividend and the bridge gets an address in every service centre. Bank your share instead, and prudence carries one winter. Let the money seep into deficits, and the queues absorb the difference, as they always have.

## AALTO

If the dividend passes, somebody must prove the retraining works, annually and in public. If consolidation wins, the analytics contracts are lucrative and your name goes on the cuts.

**The decision.** Build the evaluation regime and the guarantee gets its proof. Sell efficiency analytics and the faculty profits from the knife. Keep academic distance and publish on both, unread.

## STARTUP

Your biggest customer is about to become reliable, fast, or frozen.

**The decision.** Bid the bridge's tooling and the state becomes a customer with knowable rules, built by your juniors. Take the acquisition offer and the cap table moves abroad at the top of the market. Sit out, and you are clean and absent again.

## HVK

The Second Gate proved whatever it proved. The window to make its lessons permanent closes when the campaign starts.

**The decision.** Normalize the exercises and preparedness becomes boring, budgeted and permanent. Securitize the audits and you get money and powers fast, with a trust bill that arrives after you have left. Stand down, and the file closes until the next gate opens it.
