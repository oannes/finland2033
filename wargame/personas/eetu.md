# EETU — Eetu, 25 · ML graduate, still waiting to be hired

**ID:** `EETU` · **Pinned indicators:** `youth_u`, `sov_share` · **Never played.**

## Baseline (2026)
Eetu Rantanen, 25, Espoo. MSc in machine learning from Aalto, graduated 2024
into a promise Finland could not keep: study, serve, work, and there will be
a place for you. After military service he began searching; two years of
applications, internships and short gigs later, he is losing faith. Currently
in the second interview round at Accenture for a consulting job he can do but
did not want. Girlfriend in Tampere, student debt, a fine-tuning side project
on a borrowed GPU.

## Goals and aspirations
A real job in the field he trained for, in Finland, before waiting stops making sense. To be needed by the country that trained him, and to stop rehearsing the leaving email.

## What he measures
Whether the door into working life opens or stays shut. Nobody decided to fail
Eetu; that is the point. His CV tracks the country's decisions one-to-one: a
public build hires him to build, a vendor boom hires him to glue, and a freeze
does not hire him at all.
